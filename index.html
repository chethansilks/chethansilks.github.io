The Rich History of Handlooms

Handlooms have been a symbol of India's rich cultural tradition for centuries. Woven by skilled artisans, these fabrics are made entirely by hand, without the use of electricity. Each region of India has its unique weaving styles, and Andhra Pradesh is especially known for its elegant Uppada and pure silk sarees.

Did you know? The art of weaving in South India dates back over 3000 years, making it one of the oldest crafts still practiced today.

Our Small Handloom Industry

At Chethan Silks, located in Weavers Colony, Tadipatri, our family has been dedicated to weaving pure silk sarees for generations. Using traditional handloom techniques, we produce Uppada and other silk sarees with intricate designs and rich colors.

We offer custom colour options, customised pure silk sarees, and custom design services for bulk orders. We specialise in wholesale and retail with a focus on customer satisfaction and quality craftsmanship.
